Introduction to E-SUNSHINE E-COMMERCE WEBSITE

The Internet is one of the most valuable inventions and has increasingly become an indispensable tool, serving as the main foundation for the transmission and exchange of information worldwide. Among its many applications, E-commerce is a form of online business that uses information technology with the support of the Internet to conduct transactions such as buying, selling, exchanging, and online payments. At the same time, e-commerce is a trend of the globalized era, offering great potential for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) to generate profits and grow.

E-commerce is considered a key trend in today’s global era—a promising field for SMEs to thrive and a great opportunity for those who want to start a business with a modern model. The E-commerce business model is seen as one of the key solutions to promoting the development of a national economy. It involves conducting business online using information technology and the Internet to carry out transactions including buying, selling, exchanging, and making payments.

To provide a convenient and efficient shopping space, I have developed a project titled “E-SUNSHINE E-COMMERCE WEBSITE”, a website specializing in electronic products such as smartphones, laptops, smartwatches, and more. Enhancing the interface to be visually appealing for both users and administrators, while creating user-friendly features to simplify the shopping and payment process, has been a top priority that my team and I have focused on and developed in the best possible way.

Implementation Method

We use various web programming languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, PHP, and PHP-Laravel. Pre-built Laravel libraries are installed, such as Laravel/UI (Auth:routes), flasher-laravel, and others.

The system's data is stored in a MySQL database.

Project Title: Puzzle Game

Objective: Develop a new game based on players' preferences.

Add a feature that allows players to choose images of any idol they like. For example, if a player selects the idol "Ju Jingyi," the system will automatically update and use images of Ju Jingyi for the puzzle. Allow players to choose difficulty levels. When a player wins, celebratory effects such as music, fireworks, or confetti will appear.

Highlights and Innovations of the Project: The game allows players to restart at any time by selecting the "New Game" option, which automatically reshuffles the positions of the puzzle pieces to create a new challenge each time. Unlike traditional puzzle games that use fixed characters (e.g., Pokémon), this game enhances user engagement by enabling players to select and interact with images of their favorite idols. This personalized feature adds novelty and excitement to the gameplay experience.

Implementation Method

We use various web programming languages such as Java.

The project includes references to code from the Pokémon game.
